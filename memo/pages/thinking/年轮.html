年轮

曾经
想当然的认为

梦想美妙真切
亲人高山仰止
爱情风景如画
婚姻坚如磐石
孩子蓝色梦幻
朋友就是一辈子
…
所遇见的都是最好的

云霄，如天堂美景
海底，似童话世界
美的绚丽而不真实

春夏秋冬，岁月轮回
所有的梦想与失意
所有的幸福与痛苦
所有的汗水与泪水
所有的期待与虚空
所有的…
理想和现实的契合与冲突
刻入生命的年轮
也化作铠甲
以及
矛与盾

泥泞的过往，栉风沐雨，
未知的将来，依旧坦然，
未经历彼此的人生
无须指点
风霜雨雪浇灌的花朵，还会傲然开放

今日天涯
明日海角
我们，笑靥如花

In the past,
Assumed without careful consideration,

Dreams are splendid and vivid,
Family is revered like towering mountains,
Love is a picturesque landscape,
Marriage is as solid as a rock,
Children’s dreams are painted in blue,
Friends are for a lifetime,
…
Everything encountered is the best.

In the sky, like heavenly beauty,
Under the sea, resembling a fairy tale world,
Beautiful, splendid, yet unreal.

Spring, summer, autumn, winter, the cycle of time,
All dreams and disappointments,
All happiness and sorrow,
All sweat and tears,
All expectations and emptiness,
All…
The harmony and conflict between ideals and reality,
Engraved in the rings of life,
Transformed into armor,
As well as
Spears and shields.

The muddy past, facing the wind and rain,
The unknown future, still faced with composure,
In a life not experienced by each other,
No need for guidance,
Flowers nurtured by wind, frost, rain, and snow will still proudly bloom.

Today at the end of the world,
Tomorrow at the farthest sea,
We, smiling like flowers.
