Liver regeneration and liver cancer

Our main research direction is liver regeneration and liver cancer, trying to reveal the mechanism of tumor occurrence, development and metastasis through normal and abnormal growth.

Our Goals

  • Reveal the initial cause of tumor initiation
  • Treat before getting illness.
  • Facilitate the integration of multi-omics data for comprehensive cancer characterization.

Cancer Research

We are dedicated to making significant contributions to the understanding and treatment of cancer through advanced bioinformatics techniques.

Our Goals

  • Utilize cutting-edge bioinformatics tools to analyze large-scale cancer genomics data.
  • Identify molecular signatures and biomarkers associated with different types of cancer.
  • Facilitate the integration of multi-omics data for comprehensive cancer characterization.
  • Develop computational models to predict cancer progression and treatment response.
  • Collaborate with clinicians and researchers to translate bioinformatics findings into clinical applications.

Commitment to Open Science

We are committed to fostering an open science culture. All our bioinformatics tools, algorithms, and findings are shared with the scientific community to accelerate cancer research globally.

Network Pharmacology

At our Network Pharmacology research center, our mission is to harness the power of network-based approaches to understand the interactions between drugs and biological systems for improved therapeutic outcomes.

Our Goals

  • Apply network analysis to elucidate the complex relationships between drugs, targets, and diseases.
  • Identify novel drug candidates and repurposing opportunities through systematic network pharmacology studies.
  • Investigate the molecular mechanisms underlying drug efficacy, resistance, and adverse effects.
  • Integrate omics data to construct comprehensive pharmacological networks.
  • Collaborate with pharmaceutical companies and healthcare institutions to translate network pharmacology discoveries into therapeutic applications.

Commitment to Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration. Our research center actively collaborates with researchers, clinicians, and industry partners to foster a multidisciplinary approach to network pharmacology research.
